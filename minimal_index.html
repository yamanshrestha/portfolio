About

I study the use of large language models for network security: threat‑intelligence extraction and correlation, SOC automation, and adversarial robustness. I’m especially interested in multi‑LLM routing, evaluation under drift, and practical integrations with SIEM/SOAR pipelines.

Research Interests

  • Automated extraction of IOCs and entities from noisy sources, with confidence scoring and linking to MITRE ATT&CK.
  • Agentic/LLM workflows for log triage, investigation hypotheses, and report drafting with guardrails.
  • Adversarial evaluation of detectors under evasion, poisoning, and concept drift using real telemetry (e.g., CICIDS2017).
